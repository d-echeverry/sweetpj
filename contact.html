Frequently Asked Questions

What type of events does Sweet Potato Jones cater to?

Sweet Potato Jones caters to a diverse range of events, from intimate private parties to large corporate gatherings, weddings, and social events. No event is too big or too small for us.

Do you offer customized menus?

Absolutely! We pride ourselves on our ability to create unique, personalized menus that cater to your specific needs and preferences. We're more than happy to work with you to ensure your event is catered exactly how you envision it.

How far in advance should I book your services?

We recommend booking as early as possible, ideally at least a month in advance. However, we understand that sometimes unexpected needs arise, and we'll do our best to accommodate last-minute bookings.

Do you cater to dietary restrictions and allergies?

Yes, we take dietary needs very seriously. If you or any of your guests have specific dietary restrictions or allergies, please let us know during the planning process so we can create a menu that everyone can enjoy.

How does pricing work at Sweet Potato Jones?

Our pricing depends on several factors including the type of event, number of guests, and menu chosen. We're dedicated to providing exceptional value and will work with you to provide a detailed quote that fits within your budget.
