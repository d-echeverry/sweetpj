Who We Are

Based in the heart of Charlotte, NC, Sweet Potato Jones is a proud, Black-owned family business that has been serving up extraordinary culinary experiences for over a decade. Our roots run deep in the community, and we've cultivated our love for food and hospitality into a thriving catering service. We are committed to bringing soulful, delicious, and high-quality cuisine to tables across the region. As a family, we pour our hearts into every dish we create, ensuring each event catered by us reflects not just our professional expertise, but also our personal touch. Welcome to our family, where good food meets great company!
